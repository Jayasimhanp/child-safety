🛡️ Our Commitment to Child Safety

Hello, It's Me ("the App"), developed by helloitsme / foonkiemonkey, is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users. We have a strict zero-tolerance policy toward any content or behavior that endangers minors or exploits children in any way.

Zero Tolerance: Hello, It's Me explicitly prohibits all forms of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE), including the creation, sharing, or distribution of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM). Any user found engaging in such behavior will be immediately and permanently banned, and the matter will be reported to the appropriate law enforcement authorities.

🚫 Prohibited Content & Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited on Hello, It's Me:

  • Any sexual content, imagery, or communication involving minors
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of children or minors
  • Sharing, requesting, or distributing CSAM in any form
  • Misrepresenting age to interact with minors inappropriately
  • Any content that sexualizes, endangers, or harms children
  • Using the app to facilitate contact with minors for inappropriate purposes

⚙️ How We Enforce This Policy

  • Age verification is required during account registration
  • Our moderation team reviews flagged content and user reports promptly
  • Automated systems detect and flag potentially harmful content
  • Accounts violating child safety policies are immediately suspended
  • All confirmed CSAM incidents are reported to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant authorities
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations

🚨 How to Report a Child Safety Concern

If you encounter any content or behavior that you believe violates our Child Safety Standards, please report it immediately using any of the following methods:

📋 Compliance with Laws & Regulations

Hello, It's Me complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations, including but not limited to:

  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • PROTECT Our Children Act
  • Google Play Child Safety Standards policy
  • Applicable local laws in all regions where the app is available
